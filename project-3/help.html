What can I do with this?

There isn’t a simple answer. These systems are large, and I am still inside them.

But there are ways to look closer, ask questions, and make different choices.

Learn where things come from

• Research brand supply chains
• Look for country-of-origin labels
• Read about how materials are sourced

Support labor transparency

• Follow organizations that track labor conditions
• Pay attention to corporate accountability
• Support brands that disclose production practices

Consume differently

• Buy less, reuse more
• Repair instead of replace
• Consider secondhand options
